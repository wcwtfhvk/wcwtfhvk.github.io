MI Max2 刷机指南

2019-06-28

Hello Mi Max 2 fans,

Today I am going to Guide you on "How To Root, Flash TWRP and Stock Recovery " on Mi Max 2 with the help of Flasher Toolkit, Flasher Toolkit is a hassle-free flasher for multi-purpose tasking. To easily flash TWRP or alternative recoveries with a few clicks.

DISCLAIMER: DO IT ON YOUR OWN RISK. Flashing can potentially bricked or bootloop your device

What's new on TWRP 3.2.1-0:- Allow restoring adb backups in the TWRP GUI (bigbiff) Fix gzip backup error in adb backups (bigbiff) Fix a bug in TWRP’s backup routines that occasionally corrupted backup files (nkk71) Better support for installing Android 8.0 based zips due to legacy props (nkk71) Support vold decrypt with keymaster 3.0 in 8.0 firmwares (nkk71) Decrypt of synthetic passwords for Pixel 2 (Dees_Troy) Support newer ext4 FBE policies for backup and restore in libtar (Dees_Troy) V2 fstab support (Dees_Troy) Bring TWRP forward to android 8.0 AOSP base (Dees_Troy) Various other minor bugfixes and tweaks

Essentials: Bootloader Unlocked USB DATA cable (the one that came with your device) Before proceeding: Disable Driver Signature Enforcement in Windows 7/8/10 64-Bit Enable USB Debugging - Goto Settings > Additional settings > Developer options > USB Debugging - Enable (If you have not activated Developer options, then Goto Settings > About phone > tap MIUI version 7 times > You are now a developer) Battery is charged at least 60%

Requirements: Toolkit For Mi Max 2.zip (31.71 MB, Downloads: 3024) minimal_adb_fastboot_v1.4.2.zip (905.89 KB, Downloads: 1283) Magisk-v15.3.zip (5.76 MB, Downloads: 1553)

Using FLASHER TOOLKIT FOR MI MAX 2

  1. Download "Minimal ADB and Fastboot" and install, download "Magisk-v15.3.zip" and move to the Internal Storage of your phone

  2. Plug your device in an ON-STATE via USB cable to your PC/Laptop.

  3. Download and extract "Toolkit For Mi Max 2.zip" , and execute "Flashkit.bat" as shown below.

  4. Next, you see the menu for what you want to do, as mentioned on the highlight, Type 1 via keyboard and press enter to flash TWRP and follow the on-screen instructions.

  5. Once the flashing is done successfully, Your phone will be booted into TWRP

  6. Click on install and select the magisk-v15.3.zip file to flash

  7. After flashing zip file reboot device

That's all you have successfully flashed TWRP (custom recovery) and rooted with magisk Enjoy!

Also you can flash Stock Recovery through the Flasher Toolkit by following the simple steps mentioned in the Flasher Toolkit
